## Why ToolcallUI?

- **AI-ready layouts** – Quickly structure your application with prebuilt layouts.
- **Tool-call components** – Render charts, tables, product lists, images, and more for AI-driven experiences.
- **Copy-paste simplicity** – Components are designed to be dropped directly into your project.
- **Powered by MijnUI** – Built on top of MijnUI components for consistency, accessibility, and modern design.

Whether you’re building dashboards, assistants, or full AI-powered products, ToolcallUI helps you deliver better user experiences without reinventing the wheel.
